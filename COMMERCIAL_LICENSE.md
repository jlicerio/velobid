## What Requires a Commercial License

Examples of commercial use that require a separate license include, but are
not limited to:

- Internal use by a business, corporation, partnership, or other
  revenue-generating entity
- Offering VeloBid's functionality as part of a paid product or service
- Deploying VeloBid to serve commercial clients or projects
- Using VeloBid in a consulting or contracting business
- Hosting VeloBid as a managed service for third parties
- Integration into a commercial product, tool, or platform
- Use by government agencies or non-profit entities with paid staff

## Obtaining a Commercial License

To inquire about a commercial license, please contact:

**Email:** licensing@velobid.example

Please include a brief description of your intended use case, the number of
users or deployments, and your organization name.

Commercial license terms are negotiated on a per-entity basis. Factors
considered include deployment scale, number of users, and nature of use.
